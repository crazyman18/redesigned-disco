Mobile Legends Bang Bang

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) developed and published by Moonton. Released in 2016, the game has become popular in Southeast Asia and was among the games chosen for the first medal event esports competition at the 2019 Southeast Asian Games in the Philippines.
In July 2018, Tencent on behalf of Riot Games won a lawsuit in Shanghai against Moonton CEO Watson Xu Zhenhua for Mobile Legends' plagiarism of League of Legends, and was awarded a settlement of $2.9 million.


Mobile Legends initially had ten heroes upon its release in 2016. It later grew to 70 by November 2018, and as of May 2020, 95 heroes are currently in the live server.There are 82 heroes in Mobile Legends. The cost of each hero ranges from 6,200 to 32,000 BP. There are 400+ skin of heroes and 150+ sacred statues. There also have an Emblem: Physical, Magical, Tank, Marksman, Support, Mage, Fighter, Assassin, and Junle. Total of 9 emblem. They also give free skin.
